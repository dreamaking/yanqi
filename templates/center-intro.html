北京雁栖湖国际会展中心

北京雁栖湖国际会展中心隶属于北京控股集团，由成功接待了APEC会议的国际顶尖会展场馆国家会议中心运营管理，位于APEC峰会主会场北京怀柔区雁栖湖生态发展示范区内，距离北京市区40分钟车程，首都国际机场30分钟车程。会展中心整体建筑面积79000平方米，设有可分隔式5500平方米无柱大议会厅和2000平方米大宴会厅，2000平方米多功能厅，以及各种现代化中小型会议室70余个；配有3000平方米厨房，可同时提供 5000人高端餐饮；同时，会展中心内设有精品酒店。北京雁栖湖国际会展中心，是集会议、展览、餐饮、酒店、综合配套服务于一体的综合型会展场馆，预计于2015年6月开业。后APEC理想会议聚集之地。

Beijing Yanqi Lake International Convention&Exhibition Center owned by Beijing Enterprises Group Company Limited, operated by professional management team from China National Convention Center who was successfully run APEC 2014. It is located in Yanqi Lake scenic area, Huairou district, Beijing. With easy access by taxi the travel time is only 40 minutes from downtown and 30 minutes from Beijing Capital International Airport. Beijing Yanqi Lake International Convention & Exhibition Center overall has a total of 79,000m2 gross floor area , with a 5,500m2 flexible pillar-free Grand Conference Hall, 2,000m2 Grand Ballroom, 2,000m2 Function Hall and over 70 meeting rooms in different sizes. The 3,000m2 kitchen is equipped to provide catering services for over 5,000 people simultaneously. Beijing Yanqi Lake International Convention &Exhibition Center is the most versatile international conference venue ideal for congresses, meetings, exhibitions, banquets after APEC, will have its grand opening in June 2015.
